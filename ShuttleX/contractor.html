For Drivers

Frequently
Asked Questions

1.

What subscription plans are available?

We offer daily (USD 7.9) and monthly (USD 99.9) subscriptions that let you keep 95–100% of your ride earnings. Alternatively, you can skip the subscription and earn 90% per trip

2.

How do driver subscriptions work?

By subscribing, you keep nearly all of your earnings from every ride. Card payments incur a small processing fee (2.9–4.9% + 1 AED), while receiving USDT directly to your crypto wallet can reach 100%. Converting USDT to your local currency adds a 3–4% withdrawal fee—but no other commissions apply.

3.

What documents do drivers need?

Simply upload your ID, driver’s license, vehicle registration, and proof of insurance. These help us verify you meet all safety and legal requirements.

4.

Can I receive payments in cryptocurrency?

Absolutely. We support USDT (and other cryptocurrencies). You can keep 100% if paid in USDT directly; any currency conversion costs 3–4%.

5.

What are the benefits of joining your driver community?

You’ll access training on service standards, gain a supportive network for sharing experiences, and get quick assistance. We also reward top-rated drivers in each region with a free month’s subscription.

6.

What makes your app different from others?

Our blockchain-powered platform offers transparent earnings, flexible subscriptions, and near-total retention of your trip fares. We combine cutting-edge tech with a strong driver community to enhance every ride—for you and your passengers.

7.

How do I become a driver on your platform?

Register on our platform, choose a subscription plan, upload the required documents, and complete verification. Once approved, start earning 100% of your trip profits.

8.
